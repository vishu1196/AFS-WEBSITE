Roof Car-BMU System

The function of roof cars is to position gondolas in front of the facade. The functions of the roof car determine the range of the system. So, in order for the BMU to reach all the places that need cleaning, maintenance or repairs, roof cars need to be made to fit a particular roof. When it comes to roof cars, we offer customized solutions for sloped roofs or roofs with a high parapet. We deliver roof cars for every roof. We always account for the concerns of our customers and other relevant parties. For that reason, we want to be involved in the process as early as possible, to make sure that we can provide a solution that is able to reach the entire facade.

Monorail System

Monorail systems are suitable for both outdoor and indoor use, for example, they can be mounted on the face of the facade or inside an atrium. A monorail track can be fixed to a wall or ceiling, or etched into the building’s structure. The track can be configured horizontally, vertically or on an incline, which allows the system to cover complex routes. Both the shape and the color can be matched to the structure, integrating the system into the structure.

Power Suspended Platforms

A powered suspended platform, or façade gondola, is a motorized, temporary aerial platform used for high-rise building exterior work, including painting, window cleaning, and cladding installation. It runs on electric hoists, typically featuring a 630kg–1000kg load capacity, and offers safer, faster access compared to traditional scaffolding
