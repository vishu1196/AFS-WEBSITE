Roof Car – BMU System

Tailor-made Building Maintenance Unit solutions for high-rise and complex facade buildings — from design and fabrication to installation and long-term support.

🏗️ Roof Car BMU

What is a Roof Car BMU?

A Building Maintenance Unit (BMU) is a permanent access system installed on the rooftop of a high-rise building. The roof car travels along precision tracks on the roof, suspending a cradle or platform over the building facade — enabling safe maintenance, cleaning, and inspection at any height.

Active Facade System designs, fabricates, and installs custom BMU systems tailored to the geometry and structural requirements of each building.

  • Custom-designed roof car to suit building geometry and roof structure
  • Precision track systems for smooth, controlled horizontal movement
  • In-house factory fabrication with full quality control
  • Electrically powered hoisting for safe, efficient operation
  • Complete installation, load testing, and commissioning
  • Ongoing inspection, servicing, and emergency call-out support
  • Compliance with IS / BS standards for work-at-height equipment

Monorail System

Custom overhead monorail track systems for controlled, horizontal facade access — ideal for buildings with complex rooflines or varying setbacks.

🛤️ Monorail Track

Precision Track Movement

Monorail systems use an overhead track fixed to the building structure to allow a suspended cradle to traverse horizontally with precision. This is ideal for buildings where a traditional roof car is not feasible — such as podium levels, irregular rooflines, or cantilevered facades.

Each system is designed to the specific profile of your building, ensuring complete facade coverage in the most efficient way possible.

  • Custom monorail track design for building-specific geometry
  • Smooth, motorised horizontal traverse along fixed track
  • Secure anchoring with safety-focused structural connections
  • Compatible with single-man and two-man suspended cradles
  • Suitable for podium facades, irregular setbacks, and stepped profiles
  • End-to-end service: installation, inspection, and annual maintenance

Davit JIB System

Floor-mounted and parapet-mounted davit jib systems offering secure, flexible facade access with strong base support — ideal for buildings without a central BMU.

🔩 Davit JIB

Flexible Point-Access Solution

Davit jib systems provide localised facade access by suspending a cradle or rope access operator from a fixed jib arm. Installed at intervals around the roof parapet or floor slab, they offer building managers a flexible, cost-effective alternative to a full roof car BMU system.

We supply motorised davits, parapet davits, and roof davits — all custom-sized to the building's structural requirements.

Facade Restoration

🔧 Restoration

Repair, Protect & Restore

We carry out targeted interventions — from silicone joint replacement to full glass unit swaps — using safe at-height access and specialist materials.

  • Facade glass unit inspection and replacement
  • Stone cladding repair, re-fixing, and replacement
  • ACP panel repair and colour-matched restoration
