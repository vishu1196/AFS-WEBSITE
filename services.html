Facade Cleaning

We undertake facade cleaning contracts for high-performance buildings with glass facades or structural glazing. Our trained teams restore building exteriors to pristine condition using proven, safe methods.

💧 Water Soaking 🔫 Pressure Washing 🧪 Chemical Cleaning ♨️ Steam Cleaning
Request This Service →

Types of Facade Cleaning

Method 01

Water Soaking

A gentle, chemical-free method ideal for lightly soiled facades. Purified water is applied at low pressure to dissolve surface dust and grime without damaging coatings or seals.

  • Safe for all glass and glazing types
  • No chemicals — eco-friendly process
  • Ideal for regular maintenance cycles
Method 02

Pressure Washing

High-pressure water jets remove stubborn stains, bird droppings, algae, and deeply embedded dirt from concrete, stone, and cladding surfaces quickly and efficiently.

  • Adjustable pressure for different surfaces
  • Hot and cold water options available
  • Ideal for heavily soiled exteriors
Method 03

Chemical Cleaning

Specially formulated, surface-safe agents dissolve rust stains, efflorescence, oxidation, and biological growth on stone, brick, and metal cladding facades.

  • pH-neutral & surface-specific solutions
  • Effective on rust, moss & mineral deposits
  • Safe neutralisation and rinse-off process
Method 04

Steam Cleaning

Superheated steam penetrates pores in stone and masonry to sanitise and deep-clean without abrasion — ideal for heritage buildings and sensitive architectural surfaces.

  • Chemical-free deep sanitisation
  • Preserves surface integrity
  • Best for heritage & ornamental facades

Monorail System – Types

Type 01

Straight Monorail Track

A fixed overhead rail system installed along the roofline, allowing a suspended cradle or trolley to travel horizontally across the full width of a building facade for cleaning and maintenance work.

  • Fixed to roof parapet or structural beam
  • Supports motorised or manual trolleys
  • Ideal for rectangular building profiles
  • Load capacity up to 500 kg
Type 02

Curved / Radius Monorail

Custom-fabricated curved rail sections that follow the contour of circular or irregular building profiles, enabling continuous access around building corners and curved facades without re-rigging.

  • Custom-bent to building curvature
  • Seamless corner & radius transitions
  • Suitable for curved glass towers
  • Precision-engineered to site specifications
Type 03

Telescopic / Extendable Monorail

An extendable arm or outreach monorail that can be adjusted to reach facades with overhangs, podium setbacks, or architectural projections that a standard fixed rail cannot cover.

  • Variable outreach — 1 m to 4 m+
  • Handles setbacks & canopy overhangs
  • Folds flat when not in use
  • Motorised extension options available

Davit Jib System – Types

Type 01

Motorised Davit

An electrically powered jib arm mounted into a roof socket, used to swing a suspended cradle out over the building edge. Motorised operation allows precise positioning with minimal manual effort.

  • Electric-powered swing & outreach
  • Fits standard 76mm/100mm roof sockets
  • Safe load capacity up to 300 kg
  • Remote-controlled operation
Type 02

Parapet Davit

Designed to clamp directly onto parapet walls without the need for embedded roof sockets — making it a practical solution for buildings where roof drilling or structural modifications are not permitted.

  • No roof penetration required
  • Clamping mechanism for parapet walls
  • Quick installation & relocation
  • Ideal for heritage & retrofit projects
Type 03

Roof Davit

A permanently installed davit base socket set into the roof structure, accepting a portable jib arm that can be moved between multiple socket positions around the building perimeter as needed.

  • Permanent base — portable jib arm
  • Multiple socket positions per building
  • Stainless steel construction
  • Low visual impact when arm is removed
Type 04

Prophet Jib Arm

A lightweight, manually operated jib arm with an adjustable outreach angle, used for buildings with complex setbacks or where different horizontal reach distances are needed at different levels.

  • Adjustable outreach angle
  • Lightweight aluminium construction
  • Portable — relocates between sockets
  • Suitable for light-duty access tasks

Over time, facades suffer from wear, environmental pollutants, and structural weakening. Our bespoke restoration service addresses all these issues to restore your building's exterior and long-term integrity.

  • ✓ Water Leakage Testing

  • ✓ Facade Glass Unit Replacement
  • ✓ Stone Cladding
Request This Service →

Types of Facade Restoration

Type 01

Water Leakage Testing

Systematic testing of glazing, joints, and seals using calibrated water spray and pressure methods to precisely identify the source of water ingress in building facades.

  • AAMA & ASTM standard test procedures
  • Localised leak source identification
  • Detailed diagnostic report provided
Type 02

Waterproofing – Silicon Filling

Application of high-performance structural silicone sealants to failed joints, expansion gaps, and perimeter seals — restoring watertight integrity to curtain walls and glazing systems.

  • UV-resistant & weather-grade silicone
  • Compatible with all glazing systems
  • Prevents future water ingress
Type 03

Facade Glass Unit Replacement

Safe removal and replacement of cracked, broken, or failed IGU (insulated glass units) at height using certified access equipment and specialist glazing techniques.

  • Single & double-glazed unit replacement
  • Carried out at any height safely
  • Minimal disruption to building occupants
Type 04

Stone Cladding Restoration

Repair, re-bonding, and re-sealing of natural stone and composite cladding panels that have become loose, cracked, or stained — restoring both safety and aesthetics.

  • Crack repair & panel re-bonding
  • Colour-matched stone treatment
  • Anti-stain & protective sealing applied
