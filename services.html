Cleaning Methods
Method 01

💧 Water Soaking

Water soaking is a gentle, low-pressure pre-treatment method used to loosen and dissolve surface dirt, dust, and light pollution deposits before manual cleaning or wiping. It is the preferred first step for delicate glass facades and high-performance coatings where abrasion or high pressure could cause damage to the surface or its protective film.

  • ✓ Safe for all coated & tinted glass surfaces
  • ✓ Softens stubborn grime before wiping
  • ✓ Low water consumption process
  • ✓ No risk of surface damage or streaking
Method 02

🔫 Jet Pressure Washing

High-pressure jet washing is highly effective for removing stubborn stains, biological growth (mould, algae, lichen), paint overspray, and heavy soiling from stone, concrete, ACP, and GRC cladding surfaces. Pressure is carefully controlled and nozzle selection is matched to the surface type to ensure deep cleaning without causing erosion or joint damage.

  • ✓ Removes biological growth & heavy soiling
  • ✓ Adjustable pressure matched to each surface
  • ✓ Effective on stone, concrete, ACP & GRC
  • ✓ Fast turnaround for large facade areas
Method 03

🧪 Chemical Cleaning

Specialist cleaning chemicals are applied to target specific types of contamination that water or pressure alone cannot remove — such as mineral deposits, rust stains, efflorescence, silicone residue, and atmospheric pollution marks. Chemicals are carefully selected for compatibility with each facade material and applied at controlled dilutions to ensure effective cleaning without damaging sealants, coatings, or the substrate.

  • ✓ Targets mineral, rust & pollution stains
  • ✓ Material-safe chemical selection per facade type
  • ✓ Neutralised & fully rinsed after application
  • ✓ Safe for glass, stone, ACP & coatings
Method 04

♨️ Steam Cleaning

Steam cleaning uses high-temperature dry steam to sanitise and deep-clean facade surfaces without the need for chemicals or excessive water. The intense heat kills mould spores, bacteria, and biological growth at the root, making it the most hygienic cleaning method available. Particularly effective on glass, metal cladding, and any surface where residue-free results are essential.

  • ✓ Chemical-free & eco-friendly process
  • ✓ Kills mould, bacteria & biological growth
  • ✓ Leaves no residue on glass or metal surfaces
  • ✓ Ideal for food-grade & sensitive buildings

Restoration Services
Sealing

Silicone Pasting

Deteriorated or failed silicone sealant joints are the leading cause of water ingress in glass and ACP facades. Our technicians remove the old sealant, prepare the joint substrate, and apply high-performance structural or weatherproofing silicone — matched to the original specification — ensuring a durable, watertight bond that restores the facade's weathering performance for years ahead.

  • ✓ Full joint preparation & primer application
  • ✓ Colour-matched silicone to original spec
  • ✓ Structural & weather-seal grades available
  • ✓ Eliminates water ingress at sealant joints
Glass

Glass Lifter Device

Cracked, broken, delaminated, or failed sealed units are a safety risk and an energy efficiency problem. We carry out glass unit replacement at height using suspended access equipment, supplying and installing matching single-pane, double-glazed, or laminated safety glass panels across residential, commercial, and industrial buildings with minimum disruption to occupants.

  • ✓ Single, double & laminated glass units supplied
  • ✓ Replacement at any height via BMU or rope access
  • ✓ Panels matched to existing facade specification
  • ✓ Residential, commercial & industrial coverage
Waterproofing

Waterproofing

We conduct comprehensive water leakage surveys using the pressure method to identify exactly where water penetration is occurring, then apply targeted waterproofing solutions — from sealant application to membrane coatings — that eliminate ingress at its source. Our approach addresses both visible water damage and hidden moisture infiltration that can cause long-term structural damage if untreated.

  • ✓ Pressure-method leakage survey & diagnosis
  • ✓ Targeted sealant & membrane application
  • ✓ Addresses hidden moisture infiltration
  • ✓ Post-treatment water test certification provided
Cladding

Stone Cladding

Stone cladding panels that have cracked, debonded, or suffered movement can pose a serious public safety risk. Our team carries out a full condition survey of the stonework, identifies areas of concern, removes damaged panels safely using at-height access, and installs replacement stone or matching composite panels to restore the facade's appearance and structural integrity.

  • ✓ Full condition survey of existing stonework
  • ✓ Safe removal of debonded or cracked panels
  • ✓ Matching stone or composite replacement
  • ✓ Re-anchoring & joint sealing included
