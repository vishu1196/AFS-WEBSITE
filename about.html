About Active Facade System

Our Story

Who We Are

Active Façade Systems is a specialized service provider in the field of façade access and maintenance solutions. Based in Navi Mumbai, we are committed to delivering high-quality, reliable, and safe façade services for modern buildings and structures.

With a strong focus on precision and workmanship, we offer a complete range of façade-related services — including façade access systems, glass cleaning, glass fitting, and silicone pasting. Our expertise ensures building exteriors are visually appealing, structurally sound, and well-maintained.

Our operations are supported by a well-organized factory process, allowing us to maintain strict quality control and deliver consistent results. We combine technical knowledge with practical experience to meet the evolving demands of the construction and maintenance industry.
