Monorail System

Monorail systems use an overhead track fixed to the building structure to allow a suspended cradle to traverse horizontally with precision. Ideal for buildings where a traditional roof car is not feasible — such as podium levels, irregular rooflines, or cantilevered facades.

Each system is custom-designed to the specific profile of your building, ensuring complete facade coverage in the most efficient way possible.

  • ✓ Custom monorail track design for building-specific geometry
  • ✓ Smooth motorised horizontal traverse along fixed overhead track
  • ✓ Secure anchoring with safety-focused structural connections
  • ✓ Compatible with solo and two-man suspended cradles
  • ✓ Suitable for podium facades, irregular setbacks and stepped profiles
  • ✓ End-to-end service: installation, inspection and annual maintenance

Access System

Davit JIB System

Davit jib systems provide localised facade access by suspending a cradle or rope access operator from a fixed jib arm. Installed at intervals around the roof parapet or floor slab, they offer a flexible, cost-effective alternative to a full roof car BMU system.

We supply motorised davits, parapet davits, and roof davits — all custom-sized to the building's structural requirements.

  • ✓ Floor-mounted base with strong, reliable structural anchoring
  • ✓ Motorised and manual variants available
  • ✓ Easy rotation to cover multiple facade panels per installation point
  • ✓ Suitable for buildings where a full BMU is not viable
  • ✓ Full installation, load testing and certification included
  • ✓ Annual inspection and maintenance contracts available

Facade Cleaning

A clean building presents a more hospitable front — and a more attractive real estate prospect. Beyond aesthetics, facade cleaning removes damaging pollutants (Sulphur & Nitrogen oxides) that accelerate facade deterioration.

We undertake facade cleaning contracts for high-performance buildings with glass facades or structural glazing. Our trained teams restore building exteriors to pristine condition using proven, safe methods.
